Welcome to the country search app!

With this app, you can easily find information about countries all over the world. Simply enter the name of a country or choose a region to filter your search, and you'll receive data such as population, country name, capital city, and currency. Whether you're planning a trip or just curious about different countries, this app is a great resource for exploring the world. Let's get started!
